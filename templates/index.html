LOS Prediction/Stacking

This project predicts a patient's length of stay and discharge date based on his/her age, gender, the Service he/she is in, and the disease he/she has.

We used 8 different models to find the one that best predicts our problem. These models are the following:

Random Forest Regressor, XGBoost, Extra Trees Regressor, Decision Tree Regressor , Light GBM, MLP Regression, Suport Vector Regressor, Stacking Regressor.

However, the final model is made using Stacking regression which is combining (Random Forest Regressor, XGBoost, Extra Trees Regressor, Decision Tree Regressor, Light GBM, and SVR) N.B: For Patients less than one year old, please convert the days (age) numbers to years. For example, if a Patient has 10 days as age, do 10/365 to get his age in year.
